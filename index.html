About Moxieyaro

Emmanuel George BABALE, known professionally as Moxieyaro, is a Nigerian gospel artist who has been blessing audiences with his unique blend of alternative, soul, and Afro-gospel music since 2017.

My mission is to spread the gospel through music that touches hearts and transforms lives, combining traditional African rhythms with contemporary gospel sounds.
